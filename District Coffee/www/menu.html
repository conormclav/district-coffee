Bailies Coffee is a close-knit team of dedicated people passionate about creating world class coffee experiences and having a lot of fun along the way. They are proud to source coffee from some of the world’s best producers, and build ethical, sustainable long term relationships with coffee farming communities committed to quality coffee production. They take meticulous care, pride and attention to detail at every stage of the coffee journey from tasting, sourcing and roasting. Our Colombian, single origin espresso is available to buy from our retail shelves.

Just Live a little

granola

Jill & David started making granola from their Strangford Lough farmhouse in 2011 as something to serve with their yoghurt, which was a little tart. The business has grown from strength to strength since. We serve their gluten free triple nut granola on our brunch menu and retail packs to take home.

Michel’s

fruit & veg

Michel’s is an Ormeau Road institution. Michel can be found in the store (number 435) most morning after visiting the local farmers markets to pick up the freshest of produce. We work closely with him to ensure we offer the best of locally sourced, seasonal fruit & veg.
